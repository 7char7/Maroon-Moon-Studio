What You Don't See 


The player is a paranormal investigator that progresses through several surreal, haunted environments.
Lighting
The Hub level connects to the Forest, Apartment, and Office levels, which will each feature a different type of deadly phantom.



Controls (Keyboard):

WASD to move
Mouse to look around
Shift to sprint
Ctrl to crouch
E to interact
Scroll wheel to switch Hotbar items
Left click to use held tool primary function
Right click to use alt function of held tool
Left click while flashlight is held to switch between on, blacklight, and off
Repeatedly press right mouse click to recharge flashlight battery


Controls (Controller)

Left joystick to move
Right joystick to look around
X to interact
Left bumper to switch Hotbar items
Right trigger to use held item
Left trigger for alt use of held item
Use flashlight to swap between modes
Repeatedly press left trigger to recharge flashlight
Right bumper for flashlight stun
B to crouch


Hub Progression
	Upon loading into the game, the player will spawn inside of an elevator that leads to an abandoned room. Inside the room are three candles that can be interacted with to be lit. Once all three candles are lit, a summoning circle will appear. Walking into the center of the circle will transport the player to the ghost's mindscape. After entering the mindscape, a wisp will spawn at the entrance of the room and three doors will appear at the far side of the room, with chains indicating that they are locked. The player is able to use their flashlight stun to disperse it, and then walk past it and turn on the lights using the light switch. Once the lights are on, the first door's chains will disappear, allowing the player to go to the first level: the apartments.

Apartments Progression
	Upon entering the apartment, a small cinematic will play that will show the player a table with a cassette player and a newspaper clipping on it. After it finishes, the player is tasked with finding eight cassette tapes throughout the level. Leaving the stairwell that the player spawns in will lead into a long hallway filled with apartment rooms. Some doors are locked. 
	The first door on the left, Room 201, contains two cassette tapes. The first of which is on a countertop at the entrance of the apartment, and the second is on the floor in a room towards the back-right of the apartment next to a couch. 
	Room 202 contains another two tapes and is located on the right wall of the hallway near room 201. One of the cassette tapes can be acquired by using the flashlight stun on the wisp flying around the living room, and the next one is in the grounded cabinet closest to the fridge in the kitchen area. 
	The next room with cassette tapes is Room 207, which can be found at the end of the left hallway at the cross section. There is a painting in the living room which can be interacted with to rotate it which will unlock a door towards the back of the apartment containing a cassette tape. The other tape can be found in the cabinet inside the bathroom.
	In the hallway opposite of the one with Room 207 is Room 208. Inside of this apartment room, the first door on the right will lead to a laundry room with two washing machines. The furthest one is still on and can be interacted with in order to pop out a cassette. In the other room of the apartment, there is another flying wisp that can be stunned to drop the final cassette tape.
	After collecting all eight cassettes, you can complete the puzzle with the cassette player located at the middle of the hall. Choosing the correct story based on the newspaper clippings is the intended way to complete the puzzle. Each pair of cassettes will tell a part of the murder story, but one cassette in each pair is giving incorrect information. The player must cross reference the audio with the news evidence. The correct order is 1-4-6-7.
	After getting the correct order, a phone will appear behind the player to collect. After collecting it, there is a chase sequence where the player has to run to the end of the hall and take the door back to the hub.

Forest Progression
	The forest level's objective is to reach the car keys and then find the abandoned car afterward. The keys can be found by following the blue wisps scattered throughout the forest. After getting the keys, there will be more wisps leading to the car.

Office Progression
	In the office, there are two codes that need to be found for the player to get into the security room, which contains the final objective. The phantom in this level can be stunned temporarily with the flashlight and will also be stopped by turning the lights on, however the lights will automatically turn off after a set time.
	The first code is for a safe in the manager's room. This code can be found as a blacklight decal at one of the desk areas, there is ectoplasm dripping from the roof near this area to indicate its location. The correct code is 4-33-26. Upon opening the safe with this code, there will be another code inside of it that is 4-5-3-7. This code opens up the security room.
	Inside the security room there is a row of computers that will have their screens off at first, with one in the middle being on. Interacting with the middle computer will begin a puzzle where the computer screens will flash on  with static in a certain order. The player will need to remember the order and interact with them in that same order to proceed. After completing this sequence, the tape will drop into the middle of the room and the door back to the hub will be unlocked in the spawn area.

Finishing the Game
	After returning to the hub, the summoning circle will be green and walking into its center will finish the game.
